About

This is a simple and free, live currency converter between British Pounds (GBP) and Euros (EUR). The exchange rates are fetched from a reliable API and cached to provide you with up-to-date conversions.

The currency converter can be used on the web as well as iPhone and Android devices, where it can be added to your home screen and used as a standalone app.

How to Add to Home Screen

iPhone (using Safari):

  1. Open the currency converter website in Safari.
  2. Tap the Share button ios_share.
  3. Scroll down and tap Add to Home Screen add_box.
  4. Tap Add in the top-right corner.

Android (using Chrome):

  1. Open the currency converter website in Chrome.
  2. Tap the three vertical dots more_vert.
  3. Tap Add to Home screen.
  4. You can then name the shortcut and tap Add.
