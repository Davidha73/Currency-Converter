About Currency Converter

Currency Converter is a precision instrument for real-time currency exchange monitoring. Our platform synchronizes with global market rates and employs intelligent caching to ensure you always have access to high-fidelity financial data, even in low-connectivity environments.

Engineered for portability, this application functions seamlessly as a Progressive Web App on both iOS and Android, providing a native-tier experience without the overhead of traditional app stores.

Mobile Installation

iOS (Safari)

  1. Navigate to the platform in Safari.
  2. Tap Share ios_share.
  3. Select Add to Home Screen add_box.

Android (Chrome)

  1. Open the platform in Chrome.
  2. Tap Menu more_vert.
  3. Select Add to Home screen.
